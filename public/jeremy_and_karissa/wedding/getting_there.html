Getting There


View Larger Map

We are getting married in Neah Bay, which is ~ 6 hours drive northwest of Seattle.

Once you're in Neah Bay everything is less than a 10 minute drive from everything else.

By Plane

You probably want to fly into Seattle / Tacoma International Airport (SEA) and rent a car or carpool from there. Neah Bay is a 6 hour drive from the airport, and you'll need all 6 hours.

Carpooling

Take a look at who's coming from Seattle.

If you're coming, please let us know if you have space for anyone else or you need a ride.

Fred & Lessa will be coordinating carpool rides from Seattle, once you've left your info, they'll match you up with a ride or ridee's. You can also just give one of them a call.
